# Radio SG

> Listen to all 18 Singapore radio stations, live and free.

**Radio SG** is the simplest way to tune in to Singapore's full lineup of radio stations — all in one beautifully designed app.

Whether you're into Mandarin pop, English hits, Tamil music, Malay culture, classical symphonies, or the latest business and world news, Radio SG has you covered with 18 live-streaming stations.

---

## Features

- Stream 18 Singapore radio stations live — 987FM, Class 95, Kiss92, Gold 905, Symphony 924, UFM 100.3, Love 972, YES 933, and more
- Save your favourite stations for quick access
- Pick up right where you left off — the app remembers your last station
- Keep listening in the background while you use other apps
- Animated audio visualizer that responds to your music
- Clean, modern dark interface designed for distraction-free listening
- Offline detection so you always know your connection status

## Tech Stack

- [Flutter](https://flutter.dev) — iOS & Android
- [just_audio](https://pub.dev/packages/just_audio) — audio streaming
- [audio_service](https://pub.dev/packages/audio_service) — background playback
- [connectivity_plus](https://pub.dev/packages/connectivity_plus) — network detection
- [shared_preferences](https://pub.dev/packages/shared_preferences) — favourites & last station persistence
- [provider](https://pub.dev/packages/provider) — state management
